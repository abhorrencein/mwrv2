Where We Work

Real work.

Deep presence, not surface-level generalism. Our team has built genuine knowledge in six areas over years of field research, policy analysis, and programme evaluation. Click any sector to see what that means in practice.

About this page
01
Public Health & Nutrition
Access, quality, and equity at the scale that matters
+

Health programmes fail not because of a lack of intent but because of a lack of credible evidence about what's actually working. Our team has designed and run studies across nutrition, maternal health, NCDs, and adolescent wellbeing — with rigour built for decision-making, not just reporting.

Work our team has done
Nutrition baseline across 9 blocks in Jharkhand and Odisha — survey design, CAPI deployment, data quality, reporting
NCD tracking programme evaluation — community landscape study on non-communicable diseases, Alwar, Rajasthan
Maternal and child health baseline — RMNCH+A strategy study across low-resource settings, Odisha
Mobile Medical Unit baseline — quantitative study, field operations, and monitoring, Uttar Pradesh
Parent coaching endline — first 1,000 days programme assessment, Delhi and Bahraich UP
Mass Drug Administration study — mixed methods research to improve MDA outcomes, Uttar Pradesh

02
Agriculture & Food Systems
Markets, resilience, and rural livelihoods under pressure
+

Rural India's agricultural systems are under simultaneous pressure from climate, markets, and policy. Our team brings both quantitative field research and academic econometric depth — including peer-reviewed publications on agrarian systems and groundwater governance.

Work our team has done
Rural transformation impact study — grants evaluation for rural development, Maharashtra and Gujarat
CSR programme evaluation — agricultural livelihoods and community development assessment
Water and sanitation credit study in FPOs across 6 states — Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, UP, West Bengal, Rajasthan, Gujarat
Lead farmer platform evaluation — season-end study, Samastipur district, Bihar
Peer-reviewed field research on groundwater governance and agrarian dynamism — published in Economic & Political Weekly

Education, Skilling & Livelihoods
From learning systems to meaningful work
+

Education interventions are only as strong as their evidence base. Our team has assessed school-level innovation, early childhood development, skilling infrastructure, and youth engagement — with methods that actually measure learning outcomes, not just activity outputs.

State Skill Index development — knowledge partner for UNICEF India, data systems and monitoring
ITI grading across 6 states — Phase 2 grading of Industrial Training Institutes, Ministry of Skill Development
Early learning programme assessment — baseline study, Maharashtra
Youth engagement mapping — analysis of government and private sector initiatives, CTSI
Comprehensive school education assessment — primary through secondary including Mid-Day Meal, Madhya Pradesh government

04
Gender & Social Norms
Behaviour, power, and norms — in practice, not theory
+

Gender work that doesn't measure norms doesn't measure the right thing. Our team has designed and run studies specifically focused on how social norms shift, who they affect, and what evidence-led change actually looks like — not just counting female beneficiaries.

Work our team has done
Second Chance Education endline — vocational learning programme for women, UN Women India
Concurrent evaluation of Taaron ki Toli — adolescent girls programme, quantitative tools and field monitoring
Nanhi Kali impact evaluation — CSR programme for girl child education, Uttar Pradesh
Menstrual health baseline — MHM programme design and data collection tools
Gender and violence needs assessment — minority women across Rajasthan, Haryana, and Madhya Pradesh, UN Women
UNFPA adolescent girls endline — Sammridhhi project evaluation, Madhya Pradesh
Services applied

Governance, Policy & Markets
Institutions, incentives, and delivery that holds
+

Policy work that doesn't understand implementation is just theory. Our team brings macroeconomic analysis, institutional diagnostic capability, and the practical experience of advising government bodies — from state departments to central ministries.

Urban expansion research — analysis of 3,947 towns, spatial regression, policy document for GoI urban planning
Metro rail census and stakeholder consultations — socio-economic survey for Kanpur Metro Rail project

06
Sustainability & Climate Resilience
Systems under environmental pressure — and what holds
+

Climate resilience isn't an environmental question — it's an economic and social one. Our team has assessed how communities, programmes, and systems hold up under environmental pressure, with research spanning MGNREGA, energy transitions, and urban vulnerability.

Work our team has done
Coal mine closure impact assessment — transitioning mining communities to sustainable livelihoods, Ashoka ACPET (two rounds)
MGNREGA climate resilience assessment — how India's rural employment guarantee builds resilience to climate shocks, across 4 states
Energy transition livelihood study — assessment of interventions for coal-dependent communities
GAIL CSR impact assessment — flagship programmes across 11 states including environmental and livelihood components
